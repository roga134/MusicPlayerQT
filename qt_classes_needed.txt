
The Jukebox - Required Qt Classes

1. Account System
- QWidget / QDialog
- QLineEdit
- QPushButton
- QLabel
- QStackedWidget or QTabWidget
- QFile + QTextStream (or QDataStream)

2. Music Player
- QMediaPlayer
- QMediaPlaylist
- QAudioOutput
- QSlider
- QListView or QListWidget
- QStringListModel or QStandardItemModel
- QPushButton
- QLabel
- QGraphicsView + QGraphicsScene (or QOpenGLWidget)

3. Online “Jukebox” Synchronization
- QTcpSocket or QUdpSocket
- QHostAddress + QNetworkDatagram (for UDP)
- QDataStream
- QTimer

4. Movie Player
- QMediaPlayer
- QVideoWidget
- QMediaPlaylist
- QSlider
- QPushButton
- QLabel
- QComboBox

5. Bonus & Final Notes
- QUndoStack + QUndoCommand
- QThread (or QtConcurrent)
- QObject (signals & slots)
- QFileDialog
